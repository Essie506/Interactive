# Verification Policy

## What Verification Means

Verification helps users understand which information on a profile has been reviewed and approved by Interactive.

Verification may relate to identity, qualifications, professional registrations, business status, or other supporting evidence. Verification indicators should not be interpreted as an endorsement, recommendation, guarantee of competence, or guarantee of future conduct.

Verification only applies to information that has been reviewed and approved by Interactive.

---

## Identity Verification

Identity verification helps confirm that an account is connected to a real person.

Identity verification may require the submission of identification documents, account information, or other evidence requested by Interactive.

Identity verification does not automatically verify professional qualifications, registrations, licences, experience, or business status.

---

## Qualification Verification

Qualification verification helps confirm that a user has provided evidence supporting a professional qualification, licence, registration, certification, membership, or other professional claim.

Verification only applies to the specific qualification or professional claim that has been reviewed and approved.

Users should not imply that unverified qualifications have been verified.

---

## Business Verification

Business verification helps confirm that a business profile is connected to a legitimate organisation, company, sole trader, partnership, or other recognised business entity.

Business verification does not automatically verify individual staff members, contractors, qualifications, licences, or professional registrations associated with the business.

Separate verification requirements may apply to individuals operating within a business.

---

## Multiple Qualifications

Users may submit multiple qualifications, registrations, licences, or certifications for review.

Approved qualifications may contribute to the number displayed alongside a verification indicator.

Each qualification is reviewed individually and may be approved, rejected, removed, or require additional information.

Verification of one qualification does not automatically verify any other qualification or professional claim.

---

## Duplicate Licence Numbers

Licence numbers, registration numbers, membership numbers, certificates, and similar identifiers should only be linked to one verified account unless otherwise approved by Interactive.

Where duplicate, suspicious, disputed, or conflicting information is identified, Interactive may pause verification, request additional evidence, conduct a manual review, reject a submission, or remove a verification indicator.

---

## Manual Review

Interactive may conduct manual reviews where information is incomplete, disputed, suspicious, duplicated, inconsistent, or otherwise requires additional assessment.

Users may be asked to provide additional evidence, documentation, clarification, or identification during the review process.

Verification requests may remain pending until the review is completed.

---

## Removal of Verification

Interactive may remove, suspend, or amend verification indicators where:

* Supporting evidence is withdrawn.
* Information becomes inaccurate or outdated.
* Qualifications expire or are revoked.
* Duplicate or disputed information is identified.
* False, misleading, or fraudulent information is submitted.
* Verification requirements are no longer met.

Removal of verification does not necessarily indicate wrongdoing and may occur for administrative or evidential reasons.

---

## False Claims

Users are responsible for ensuring that qualifications, registrations, licences, business information, professional titles, and profile claims are accurate.

Users should not claim qualifications, registrations, licences, memberships, professional titles, experience, or business status that they cannot reasonably support with evidence.

Interactive may remove verification indicators, restrict account features, suspend accounts, or take other appropriate action where false, misleading, fraudulent, or unsupported claims are identified.

## Unique Qualifications and Registrations

A qualification, registration, licence number, membership number, certificate, or similar professional identifier may only be linked to one verified Interactive account at any given time unless otherwise approved by Interactive.

Where the same qualification or registration information is submitted by multiple accounts, verification may be paused while ownership is reviewed.

Interactive may request additional evidence to establish ownership before granting, transferring, or restoring verification.
