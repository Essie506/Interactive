Interactive Terms & Policies

These sections explain how Interactive works, what users can expect, and how verification, reporting, blocking and personal data are handled.

Verification Terms

Verification is linked to qualifications, professional registration, business status, or identity checks. Users may submit multiple qualifications for review. Verified indicators should only relate to claims supported by approved evidence.

Duplicate, suspicious, or disputed licence numbers may be paused, rejected, removed, or reviewed manually.

Personal Data

Interactive may collect account details, profile information, verification evidence, uploaded media, messages, reports and safety information where needed to operate the platform.

Some information may be private, some may appear publicly on profiles, and some may only be used for review, verification, safety or legal purposes.

Reporting and Blocking

Users may report accounts, content, behaviour, or suspected misuse. Interactive may review reports and take action where needed, including warnings, restrictions, removal of content, or account suspension.

Blocking helps users control who can contact or interact with them. Blocking does not prevent Interactive from reviewing safety issues where a report has been made.

Professional Conduct

Professionals and businesses should only advertise services they are suitably qualified, insured, experienced or authorised to provide. Claims made on profiles should be accurate and not misleading.

Content and Uploads

Users are responsible for the content, images, documents and media they upload. Content should not be misleading, unsafe, abusive, stolen, or infringe another person’s rights.
